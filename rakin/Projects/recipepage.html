Pizza

Pizza is an International Dish. Its Origin is from Italy.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sausage Slice

Instructions

  1. Make a Dough with All Purpose Flour Then Make Round Pizza Shape.
  2. Put Sauce on it.
  3. Put Cheese Slices on it all over.
  4. Put Sausage Slices Then Bake it on Oven.
